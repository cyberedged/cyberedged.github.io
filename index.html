About Us

CyberEdged provides a variety of specialized tactical and strategic services to meet our customers security needs. Our consultants are Azure, CISSP, CISA, CISM, OSCP, and SANS certified and have over 20 years experience in IT and Security. Best practices and lessons learned from regulated industries are incorporated into our security assessments and programs. As the threat landscape continues to evolve rapidly, our mission is to ensure your organization is proactively identifying and preventing current and emerging threats.

We strive to be an extension of your group and want to help you secure your organizations future.

Services

CyberEdged provides a comprehensive suite of specialized services that will enhance an organization's security posture and reduce overall risk in the environment.

Threat Intelligence

CyberEdged constantly monitors the threat landscape and researches, tracks, and analyzes new threats. We deliver actionable and insightful information and our products are tailored and aligned to an organization's specific threat landscape, its industry and sector.

Incident Response

CyberEdged can identify, contain, investigate, and remediate a cybersecurity incident, such as a data breach, network intrusion, or malware attack using expert analysis and lessons learned from various cyber incidents.

Security Assessments

CyberEdged performs technical assessments that identify potential weaknesses associated with known vulnerabilities and misconfigurations. The findings are analyzed, quantified, and prioritized for remediation based on compensating controls.

Security Programs

CyberEdged help evaluate the effectiveness of an organization's information security management program. We provide assessments that are tailored and aligned to an organization's specific threat landscape, its industry and sector.

Threat Hunting

CyberEdged leverages cyber-threat intelligence and employs specialized data science tools, exploratory techniques, and pattern discovery to hunt for potential unknown compromises within internal networks and leaked company data in external data dumps.

Trusted Advisory

CybeEdged is an extension of your organization that supports and provides guidance on various strategic objectives. We provide a diverse suite of security advisory services that can be leveraged by organizations to meet their specific needs.
